About

Archana Bennur

Born into a family of traditional classical musicians, where the heritage of authentic classical music has been passed from generation to generation, Archana is the granddaughter of Swaramurthy V.N. Rao – grandson of Vainika Shikhamani Veena Sheshanna, the well known erudite musician of Mysore.



Highlights

Numerous Karnatak Classical performances in various Television Channels and on All India Radio.
M.M. (Master of Music), College of Fine Arts, University of Mysore,Karnataka, INDIA, Gold Medal (for First rank - Highest marks for both year I and II).
B.M.(Bachelors of Music) Madras University, Chennai, INDIA.
Graded Artist from All India Radio.

Training in Music

She had the initial training from her mother Dr. Rama V. Bennur and further training under Vid. Neela Ramgopal , Vid. Rajashree Aravind and also from Dr.R.N. Srilata. She also learnt from Vid. S. Sowmya at Chennai and is currently learning from Vidushi. Koviladi Kala .

Awards and Recognition

  • Is a recipient of “Aryabhata Award-2003”which is awarded for outstanding Young artistes.
  • She was awarded the"Bharatha Bharathi Puraskara" for her performance at the Young Artists Music Festival, held by Malleswaram Sangeetha Sabha-Bangalore [2000].
  • She was awarded the ‘Pratibhakankshi Award’ for her outstanding performance at the well known Sri Ramanavami Sangeetotsava held at Sri Rama Seva Mandali, Chamarajpet, Bangalore [2003].
    She has received senior level music scholarship from the ‘State Sangeetha Nrithya Academy’ twice.
  • First prize in the senior level classical music competition conducted by the fine arts organization ‘Sourabha’ –Bangalore [1998].
  • First prize in the state level competition on ‘Annamacharya’s compositions’ conducted by the organization ‘Nadopasana’of Tirumala Tirupathi Devasthanam-TTD-[2000].
  • First prize in the state level classical music competition conducted by Indian Institute of Music and arts.
  • First prize in the television based music competition ‘Sangeetha Sambhrama’, conducted by Doordarshan, Bangalore.
  • Second prize in the state level concert competition - Nadasudhaa Shimoga - Karnataka [2000]
  • Second prize - D.K. Jayaraman Memorial competition of Chennai held at Bangalore [2001].

She also won several other prizes in competitions conducted by prestigious organizations such as Bharatiya Vidya Peetha, Vasavi Vidya Peetha, Vijayanagar Sangeetha Sabha, etc.
She has won the First Prize [Karnatic Senior Vocal] in the All India competition held by Navya Nataka Samithi in Hyderabad and had the opportunity to sing and receive the trophy from the music Maestro Dr. M.Balamuralikrishna [2004].
